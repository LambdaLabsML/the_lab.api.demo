Plan an ordered sequence of inputs. Actions apply to whichever piece is currently falling. $ACTION_LEFT$/$ACTION_RIGHT$ slide one column; $ACTION_ROT_RIGHT$ rotates 90° clockwise; $ACTION_ROT_LEFT$ rotates 90° counter-clockwise; $ACTION_COMMIT$ hard-drops the current piece and locks it instantly (the next piece then spawns and subsequent actions in your list apply to IT — i.e. you can plan across multiple pieces in one call).
